Privacy Policy

Prompt Manager ("we," "our," or "the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Prompts and Metadata: The prompts you create (title, content, category, tags) and timestamps.

Storage Location: All data is stored locally on your device (UserDefaults / on-device storage).

Optional Sharing: If you choose to open/share a prompt (e.g., to a browser or another service), the prompt content you select is shared as part of that user-initiated action.

How We Use Your Information

  • We store and display your prompts for your personal use.
  • All processing is on-device; we do not run a backend for your data.
  • Sharing/opening a prompt to the web is only performed when you explicitly initiate it.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data remains on your device.
  • Deleting prompts or uninstalling the app removes stored data.
  • There is no server-side storage.

Permissions

  • The App does not require special device permissions beyond standard app functionality.
  • Any optional sharing is triggered only when you choose to share/open a prompt.

Your Rights

  • Add, edit, and delete your prompts at any time.
  • Uninstalling the App deletes all stored data.
  • Control sharing by only initiating it when you choose.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, share, or trade your personal data.
  • If you choose to open/share a prompt to an external site or service, that service's privacy policy applies to what you share.

International Data Transfers

  • We do not transfer your data; it stays on your device.
  • Any user-initiated sharing to external services may involve their processing under their policies.

Compliance

Designed to align with GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, and Apple App Store Privacy Guidelines given on-device-only storage and user-initiated sharing.
